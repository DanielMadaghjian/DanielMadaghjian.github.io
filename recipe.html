Pizza

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Cheese 20g
Flour 30g

Step 1: Make a well in the center of the dough

Step 2:Knead the dough and cover in plastic wrap

Step 3:Divide and shape the dough and top the pizza and bake

Lasagne

  • Time: 40 minutes
  • Serves: 3
Ingredients Quantity
butter 75g
milk 1 L

Step 1:Cook 750 g of mince

Step 2: Make sauce with 800g passata and 200ml hot beef stock.

Step 3: Cook until you have 3 layers of pasta

Step 4: Bake for 45 minutes
